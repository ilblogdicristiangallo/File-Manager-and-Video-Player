# File-Manager-and-Video-Player
The ultimate File Manager &amp; Video Player for Fire TV. Browse Internal &amp; USB storage (FAT32/exFAT) with full read/write access. Includes a powerful built-in Video Player, a Music Player with Visualizer, and a ZIP extractor. Features a clean, TV-optimized grid interface with D-Pad support. No ads, no account needed.

Unlock the full potential of your Fire TV with "File Manager & Video Player".

This is not just another file explorer; it is a comprehensive media center designed specifically for the big screen. Whether you want to manage your internal storage, organize files on your USB drive, or play your favorite movies and music, this app does it all with a clean, remote-friendly interface.

# 🔥 KEY FEATURES:

📂 Advanced File Management
Take full control of your storage. Copy, Move, Rename, and Delete files and folders with ease. The app features a grid-based layout optimized for D-Pad navigation, making it incredibly fast to browse through thousands of files using just your remote control.

💾 Full USB Read & Write Support
Unlike many other apps, we offer true Read and Write access to external USB drives (FAT32 & exFAT). You can transfer files from your TV's internal memory to your USB drive and vice versa.
(Note: Fire TV Stick users require an OTG cable).

🎬 Powerful Video Player
Watch your movies and home videos without needing external players.

Format Support: Plays MKV, MP4, AVI, MOV, WEBM, and many more.
Smart Controls: Features "YouTube-style" seeking (hold Left/Right to scrub), Fit/Fill screen modes, and a distraction-free overlay.
🎵 Music Player with Visualizer
Turn your TV into a stereo system. Play your MP3, FLAC, and AAC files with a stunning, real-time Audio Visualizer that reacts to the beat of your music, creating a classic media player experience.

📦 Tools & Utilities

ZIP Extractor: Unzip archives directly on your device.
Multi-Selection: Select multiple files to delete them in bulk.
Clipboard: Copy files from one folder and paste them into another seamlessly.
📺 Designed for TV

No mouse required.
Fully optimized for Fire TV Remote navigation.
Clean, modern interface with file type icons and storage details.
🔒 Privacy Focused

100% Offline: No internet connection required.
No Accounts: Just install and use.
No Data Collection: Your files stay on your device.
Supported Devices: Fire TV Stick (Lite, 4K, 4K Max), Fire TV Cube, and Fire TV Edition Smart TVs.
