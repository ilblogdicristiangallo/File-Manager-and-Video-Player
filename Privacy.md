# Privacy Policy for File Manager & Video Player
This application ("File Manager & Video Player") respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data or usage information.

1. Data Collection
This app operates entirely offline on your device. It does not connect to external servers, does not use analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase), and does not contain third-party advertising SDKs. No data leaves your device.

2. Permissions
The app requests specific permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

Storage Access (Read/Write): Required to list, copy, move, and delete files on your internal storage and connected USB drives.
Audio Recording (Microphone): Required solely by the Android system to generate the real-time music visualizer effect. No audio is ever recorded, saved, or transmitted.
USB Host: Required to detect and access external USB storage devices.
3. Children’s Privacy
Our app does not collect any personal information from children or any other users.
